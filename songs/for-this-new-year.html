For This New Year

A Prayer at the Beginning of the Year

Let us then approach the throne of grace with confidence, so that we may receive mercy and find grace to help us in our time of need.
Hebrews 4:16
Downloads

As we enter into each new year, be it the calendar year, a new "program year" in our congregations, or a new year in our personal or family lives, what can we do better than to stop and lift our voices in prayer for the grace to make the new year all that God would have it be.

May God bless us all with his grace, his love and his strength in the year ahead.
Sandy
